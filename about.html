Our History

The school was founded in 1967, inspired by the Uganda Martyrs of 1886. The visionaries behind the school included Late Msgr. Lawrence Mbwega, Jeremiah Munyigwa, Hellen Namazzi, and Joseph Mugerwa. Over the decades, it has grown into a center of academic excellence.

Our Mission

To develop academic and other talents of students in a truly humane and Christian environment in a Catholic Tradition.

Our Vision

To be the best center of holistic learning at secondary education with a global impact.

Our Core Values

  • Prayer
  • Honesty
  • Thinking Big
  • Respect and Obedience
  • Generosity & Networking
